# Show Finder

Discover upcoming concerts and performances from the artists you already love.

## How it works

Sign in with your Spotify account and Show Finder pulls your most listened to artists. Browse your personalized lineup, click any artist, and instantly see their upcoming shows and tour dates powered by the Ticketmaster API.

## Features

- Spotify OAuth login
- Personalized artist lineup based on your listening history
- Live concert and tour data via Ticketmaster
- Filter performances by location
